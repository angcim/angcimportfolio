GET TO KNOW ME

BACKGROUND

I was born in the Philippines but I came to California when I was 6 years old and have lived in Los Angeles for most of my life. I moved to Northern California to go to University of California, Davis. After graduating in 2019 with a Bachelor's in Design and Minor in Professional Writing, I packed my bags and moved to Orlando, Florida for a new adventure.

HOBBIES AND INTERESTS

My interests include watching classic and silent films and binge-watching tv shows. As a science fiction lover, my favorite movies are Back to the Future and Metropolis. I'm also a sucker for good fantasy like Lord of the Rings (which is the best trilogy ever created). It is my precious haha. When it comes to music, I love classic rock and metal. Some of my favorite bands are Iron Maiden, Metallica, Megadeth, and Judas Priest. I am a makeup enthusiast and a fashionista who loves to dress in style and on a budget.
